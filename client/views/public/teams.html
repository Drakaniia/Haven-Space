OUR MISSION

Haven Space bridges the gap between boarders and boarding house owners through a seamless digital platform. We empower students to find verified, affordable living spaces near their schools—while giving landlords tools to manage properties efficiently.

01 transparency in every listing
02 trust through verified properties
03 community over transactions

CHALLENGES WE OVERCAME

01

Limited Online Presence

Many boarding houses lacked visibility. We built a centralized platform where owners can list and boarders can discover spaces effortlessly.

02

Manual Inquiry Process

Traditional boarding searches relied on phone calls and physical visits. We digitized the entireprocess—application, verification, and communication.

03

Trust Deficiency

Boarders often doubted listing authenticity. We implemented verification workflows and owner reviews to establish credibility in every transaction.

04

Fragmented Management

Landlords juggled multiple channels for inquiries and payments. Our unified dashboard consolidates everything into one streamlined experience.
